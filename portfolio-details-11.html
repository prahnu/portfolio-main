“Before You Begin” Process Infographic

This infographic presents a clear, step-by-step guide outlining the essential actions to complete before initiating a key process or project. The design features a dark background with five vertically arranged steps, each accompanied by a distinct icon and sequential numbering for easy navigation.

  • Step 1: Microsoft Dynamics – Initiate the process within the Microsoft Dynamics platform.
  • Step 2: Complete PACE – Ensure all PACE requirements are fulfilled.
  • Step 3: Prepare a Pricing Plan – Develop a comprehensive pricing plan tailored to the engagement.
  • Step 4: Draft and sign an Engagement Agreement – Formalize the engagement by drafting and signing the necessary agreement.
  • Step 5: Open an engagement code – Generate and assign an engagement code to track and manage the project.

The left side of the infographic prominently displays the phrase “BEFORE YOU BEGIN,” emphasizing that these preparatory steps are prerequisites for starting the main process.
I enhanced this graphic by making it interactive using HTML, CSS, SVG, and JavaScript—when users click on each step, relevant content opens dynamically, providing a seamless and engaging experience.

This visual guide is ideal for onboarding, training, or process documentation, providing users with a concise and actionable checklist to ensure readiness before proceeding.
